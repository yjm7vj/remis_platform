Welcome to Remis Pizzeria and Lounge

Remis Pizzeria and Lounge is built for Martinsburg: a neighborhood pizzeria, lounge, and convenience stop where guests can grab fresh pizza, shawarma, wings, burgers, drinks, and everyday essentials. We are proud to continue the legacy of Bob's Carry Out by keeping local service, familiar flavors, and community care at the center of what we do.

Our Community Promise

Remis Pizzeria and Lounge is here to be a reliable neighborhood place for Martinsburg: somewhere families can count on for fresh food, quick essentials, friendly faces, and service that feels personal. We are proud to continue the Bob's Carry Out legacy by welcoming longtime customers, new neighbors, workers on a lunch break, families picking up dinner, and anyone who needs a good meal made with care. Our promise is to serve the community with respect, consistency, and warmth, to keep listening to what our neighbors need, and to build a place on N Raleigh Street where people feel valued every time they walk through the door.
