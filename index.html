Welcome to Remis Pizzeria and Lounge

Remis Pizzeria and Lounge proudly serves the Martinsburg, WV community with fresh pizza, authentic shawarma, wings, burgers, gyros, pasta dishes, sandwiches, and more. Built on the legacy of Bob's Carry Out, we are committed to quality food, friendly service, and a welcoming place where family, friends, and neighbors can come together. Whether you are dining in, ordering carryout, or stopping by for convenience store essentials, Remis is proud to continue a trusted local tradition.
